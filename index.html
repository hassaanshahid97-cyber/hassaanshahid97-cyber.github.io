01 / About

At the intersection of simulation and real-world flight

Aerospace Engineer with hands-on experience in UAV systems engineering, autonomous flight control, and propulsion performance — currently pursuing an MS in Aeronautical Engineering at Linköping University (LiU), Sweden.

My work sits where modelling meets the flight line. I design, model, and validate aerial platforms using MATLAB/Simulink, ANSYS, and CATIA V5, and prove them out through rigorous SIL/HIL pipelines before they ever leave the ground. I've built and flown fixed-wing UAVs, led multidisciplinary competition teams, and founded an aerospace startup focused on propulsion-integrated UAV systems.

I'm currently open to entry- to mid-level roles in Aeronautical Engineering, UAV Systems Engineering, Flight Control, or Systems Integration — based in Linköping and open to hybrid and on-site opportunities across Europe.

What I bring to a team

  • → End-to-end UAV dev: concept → CAD → CFD → fabrication → flight test
  • → Flight control: PID/LQR design, state-space modelling, ArduPilot/Pixhawk
  • → Simulation: SIL/HIL/IronBird testbed design & validation
  • → Composite airframe manufacturing & propulsion optimisation
  • → Leadership in cross-functional, time-critical engineering teams
